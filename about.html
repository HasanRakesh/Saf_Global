About Us

Who Are We

SAF Global Maritime Pvt. Ltd. is a world-class Marine Logistics transportation company. We specialize in transporting all kinds of goods across the world via air, water, and road mediums.
We are operating all over the world with numerous transport offices across international borders. SAF Global is committed to providing the best services to its clients and to do so, we have created an effective network that ensures stable, reliable, and time-bound services. 

Shipping logistics made easy

Our Mission

Our mission at SAF Global is to become a global leader in the shipping and transportation industry. We want to revolutionize the shipping and transportation network by creating policies of excellence, service, efficiency, and innovation.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the go-to online platform for businesses and individuals worldwide seeking secure, efficient, and convenient notary services, enabling them to conduct important transactions and sign documents in minutes from anywhere, anytime..
